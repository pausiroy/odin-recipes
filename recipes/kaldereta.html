Kaldereta

Description

Beef Kaldereta is a main stay in any Filipino Kitchen. It is a type of beef stew cooked with tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs beef cubed
  • 3 pieces garlic cloves crushed and chopped
  • 1 piece onion finely chopped
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 piece red bell pepper
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon chili flakes
  • 3 pieces dried bay leaves
  • 2 cups potatoes sliced
  • 2 cups carrots sliced
  • 1/4 cup cooking oil
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Pour the water in a pan and bring to a boil. Add salt and pepper.
  2. Heat the cooking oil in the cooking pot or pressure cooker
  3. Saut√© the onion and garlic.
  4. Add the beef. Cook for 5 minutes or until the color turns light brown.
  5. Add the dried bay leaves and crushed pepper. Stir.
  6. Pour-in the tomato sauce and beef broth
  7. Cook the beef until it becomes tender (about 30 mins if using a pressure cooker, or 1 to 2 hours if using an ordinary pot).
  8. Add potato and carrots. Cook for 8 to 10 minutes.
  9. Add salt and pepper to taste
  10. Serve hot. Share and Enjoy!
